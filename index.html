Cristiano Ronaldo

Who is he?

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. Born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal, he has played for top clubs like Sporting Lisbon, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and now Al-Nassr in Saudi Arabia. Ronaldo is known for his incredible goal-scoring ability, speed, athleticism, and versatility on the field. He has won numerous awards, including five Ballon d'Or trophies, and holds multiple records, including the most goals in UEFA Champions League history. Off the field, he's also a global icon with a massive social media following and numerous endorsements.

Cristiano Ronaldo's Achievements

Here is a list of Ronaldos Achievements thorughout his career as a pro footballer.

Team Achievements -

  • 5x UEFA Champions League titles: 2007–08, 2013–14, 2015–16, 2016–17, 2017–18
  • 7x League titles
    • 3x Premier League: 2006–07, 2007–08, 2008–09
    • 2x La Liga: 2011–12, 2016–17
    • 2x Serie A: 2018–19, 2019–20
  • 4x FIFA Club World Cup titles: 2008, 2014, 2016, 2017
  • 3x UEFA Super Cup titles: 2014, 2016, 2017
  • 3x National Cups
    • FA Cup: 2003–04
    • Copa del Rey: 2010–11, 2013–14
  • UEFA European Championship: 2016 (with Portugal)
  • UEFA Nations League: 2019 (with Portugal)

Individual awards-

  • 5x Ballon d'Or: 2008, 2013, 2014, 2016, 2017
  • 4x European Golden Shoe: 2007–08, 2010–11, 2013–14, 2014–15
  • Top Scorer in UEFA Champions League: 7 seasons
  • FIFA Best Men’s Player: 2016, 2017
  • PFA Players' Player of the Year: 2006–07, 2007–08
  • FIFA Puskás Award: 2009 (for best goal)
  • Premier League Golden Boot: 2007–08
  • La Liga Best Player: 2013–14
  • Serie A Player of the Year: 2019, 2020

Records

  • Most goals in UEFA Champions League history: 140+ goals
  • Most international goals: 123+ goals (as of 2024)
  • Most goals in a single UEFA Champions League season: 17 (2013–14)
  • Most career hat-tricks: 60+
  • Most appearances in UEFA Champions League: 180+
